At one time, the World-Honored One was at Savatthi, at Jetavana, in the garden of Anathapindika (Anathapindika).
Here, the World-Honored One called the monks:
"Hey monks".
-- "World-Honored One", those monks answered the World-Honored One.
The World-Honored One said as follows:
"Monks, I will teach you the dharma of the forest.
Listen and consider carefully, I will explain."
--"Yes, Venerable One."
Those monks answered yes to the World-Honored One.
The Buddha preached as follows:
