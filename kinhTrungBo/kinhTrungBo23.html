Buddha, what is a termite mound, what smokes at night, what shines during the day, who is a Brahmin, who is a wise man, what is a sword, what is a dig, what is a door latch, what is a frog, what is a two-way road, what is a milk purifier, what is a turtle, what is a cleaver, what is a piece of meat, what is a cobra?

Monks, a wise person is synonymous with a learned monk.
