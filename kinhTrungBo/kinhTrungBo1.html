At one time the World-Honored One lived at Ukkattha, in the Subhaga forest (Forest of Happiness), under the Sala king tree.
At that place, the World-Honored One called the monks:
"Hey monks!".
- "World-Honored One", those monks answered the World-Honored One.
The Ton said:
"Hey monks.
I will teach you "The Dharma that is the basis of all Dharmas".
Listen and pay attention, I will speak."
- "Yes, World-Honored One", those Bhikkhus answered the World-Honored One.
The World-Honored One said as follows:

Monks, the Tathagata is an Arhat, fully enlightened, and knows the earth element as the earth element.
Because he clearly knows the earth element as the earth element, the Tathagata does not think of the earth element, does not think of (self) in comparison with the earth element, does not think of (self) as the earth element, does not think:
"The earth element is mine" - no desire or joy of the earth element.
Why is that?
Because Tathagata knows that:
"Desire is the basis of suffering, from existence, birth arises, and aging and death come to living beings."
Therefore, monks, I say because the Tathagata, with the complete destruction of cravings, the dispassion, the cessation, the renunciation, the complete renunciation of cravings, has attained true and unsurpassed enlightenment.
