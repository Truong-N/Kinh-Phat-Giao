Bhikkhus, in the Dharma and Vinaya, if there is pure faith in the Teacher, that pure faith is considered incomplete;
if there is pure faith in the Dharma, that pure faith is considered incomplete;
if there is perfect attainment of the precepts, that attainment is considered incomplete;
if there is affection for all phenomena, that affection is considered incomplete.
Why is that?
Bhikkhus, the situation is as follows: In a Dharma and Vinaya that is not skillfully expounded, not skillfully displayed, not leading to higher states, not leading to tranquility, and not displayed by a Perfectly Enlightened Buddha.
